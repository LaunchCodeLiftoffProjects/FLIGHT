Welcome to the Body Mass Index calculator.

BMI is a measure of body size and is useful in determining overweight and obesity, although it is not a perfect system. Your value is based off of the mass and height of your body. Below, please enter your height in feet/inches and your weight in pounds. Once you receive your results, you can find the corresponding value in the chart below the calculator to give you some insight on where to take your health journey next.
