An integral piece of staying fit and healthy is to get your body moving. While a full on exercise routine sounds great, we do not want to discount the benefit of simply walking! Walking a few times a week for 20-30 minutes can greatly improve respiratory function as well as drop some pounds at a safe pace when coupled with a healthy diet. Plus, if you have a four-legged walking companion, they would benefit as well. It's a win-win for everyone! Use this page as a resource to find walking trails.
